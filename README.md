# Volunteer Connect

A centralized web-based platform that connects volunteers with NGOs, orphanages, and social organizations. The system simplifies volunteering activities, improves coordination, and provides verified digital certificates to recognize volunteer contributions.

The Unified Volunteering Portal is designed to bridge the gap between volunteers and organizations by offering a transparent, efficient, and certificate-driven volunteering ecosystem. It allows organizations to post volunteering opportunities and enables volunteers to apply, participate, and receive recognition through digitally verifiable certificates.
